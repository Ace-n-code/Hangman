Hangman Game Overview

How to play:

  • The computer randomly selects a word from a predefined list.
  • The player is shown a series of blank spaces representing the letters of the word.
  • For each correct guess, the letter is filled in the word.
  • For each wrong guess, a part of hangman is drawn.
  • The goal is to guess the word before the hangman is fully drawn.

Rules:

  1. The guesser suggests one letter at a time.
  2. Correct guesses are filled in; wrong guesses lead to drawing the hangman.
  3. The guesser wins by guessing the word; they lose if the hangman is completed.
